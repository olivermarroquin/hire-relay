# Product Brief

## What This Is

A lightweight decision layer between startups and their external recruiters — not an ATS replacement, but the missing infrastructure between email and full HR tooling.

## Core Value Proposition

Startups working with external recruiters lose strong candidates not from sourcing failures, but from stalled internal decisions. This product removes that friction with structured, one-click review tracking.

## The Problem

Most early-stage startups run hiring out of email:
- Recruiter submissions arrive as attachments in inboxes
- Feedback and status live in threads
- Hiring managers lose context across Slack and email
- Decisions that should take hours stretch into days
- Strong candidates disengage or accept competing offers

The gap isn't sourcing. It's decision velocity.

## The Solution

A collaboration layer with three core surfaces:

1. **Submission** — Recruiters submit candidates via a shareable link or web form. No account required.
2. **Review** — Hiring managers receive a one-click review link. Interview / Hold / Reject with optional notes.
3. **Tracking** — Status is always visible. Decision history is logged. Everyone stays aligned.

Email becomes notification only. The pipeline becomes the system of record.

## What This Is Not

- Not an ATS
- Not a sourcing tool or recruiter marketplace
- Not an AI workflow engine
- Does not replace internal applicant tracking
- Does not touch existing HR systems

## Target Customer

Seed to Series B startups that:
- Work with one or more external recruiters
- Do not want to implement a full ATS
- Experience decision lag between submission and response
- Value speed over process overhead
- Have 10–150 employees, 2–8 open roles at a time

## Positioning

For startups that rely on external recruiters, this is the lightweight collaboration infrastructure that sits between email and a full ATS — designed for decision velocity, not HR compliance.

## Validation Status

Pre-revenue. Building MVP to validate demand with 2–5 pilot companies.
